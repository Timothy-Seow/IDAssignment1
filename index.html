Cyberpunk is a single-player open-world action based game. It all begins in V city where you play as A, a professional mercenary seeking for a way to be immortal.

Player's Unknown Battleground is a multiplayer online battle royale that places you into a battlefield with 99 other players. Search for supplies, outsmart your opponents and become the last one standing.

Phasmophobia is a 4 player online co-op game where you and your team have to obtain as much evidence as you can with the use of ghost-hunting equipments.

Among Us is a 4-10 player online game filled with teamwork and betrayals. Gain trust from your teammates or you will be disposed.

Rust is a survival-based game where you have to gather resources youself and overcome situations like hunger and danger. Shelter yourself from danger, protect youself from enemies and survive.
